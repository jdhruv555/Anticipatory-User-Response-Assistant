### To Make It Private (Recommended):

1. Go to: https://github.com/jdhruv555/Anticipatory-User-Response-Assistant/settings
2. Scroll down to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Change visibility"
4. Select "Make private"
5. Type repository name to confirm
6. Click "I understand, change repository visibility"

### Benefits of Private Repository:
- ✅ Only you and collaborators can access
- ✅ Prevents unauthorized copying
- ✅ Full control over who sees the code
- ✅ Can still share with specific people via invitations
